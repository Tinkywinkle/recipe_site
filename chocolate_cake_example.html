Chocolate Cake Recipe

" Here is a fast and easy chocolate cake recipe for you. Enjoy! "

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Time Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, melted butter and melted chocolate.

2

Add eggs and mix together for 1 minute.

3

Pour into a lined cake tin and bake for 25 - 35 minutes at 160C.

4

If the cake springs back it's cooked. Do not overcook.

5

Ice cake once it has cooled.
